# EcoSpark 🌱 - Gamifying Environmental Action

## 🌍 Overview
EcoSpark is a cutting-edge, gamified web application designed to inspire and educate users—primarily students—to take real-world environmental actions. By combining AI-powered verification, interactive learning, and competitive leaderboards, EcoSpark transforms sustainability from a chore into a rewarding journey.

---

## 🛠️ The Tech Stack

### **Frontend**
- **Framework**: [React.js](https://reactjs.org/) (built with [Vite](https://vitejs.dev/))
- **Styling**: Vanilla CSS with modern **Glassmorphism** aesthetics.
- **Animations**: [Framer Motion](https://www.framer.com/motion/) for smooth, fluid transitions.
- **Charts**: [Chart.js](https://www.chartjs.org/) for visualizing user progress and environmental impact.

### **Backend & Infrastructure**
- **BaaS (Backend as a Service)**: [Firebase](https://firebase.google.com/)
- **Authentication**: Firebase Auth (Email/Password & Google Login).
- **Hosting**: Firebase Hosting for high-speed global delivery.

### **Database**
- **NoSQL Database**: [Cloud Firestore](https://firebase.google.com/docs/firestore) for real-time data persistence (user profiles, points, badges, and progress).

### **Artificial Intelligence (The Brain)**
- **API**: [Google Gemini API](https://ai.google.dev/) (Model: `gemini-1.5-flash`).
- **Use Cases**:
    - **Eco-Buddy**: A real-time AI mascot/chatbot that answers environmental questions and encourages users.
    - **Plant Verification**: Advanced Computer Vision used to verify if a user has actually planted a real tree sapling before awarding points.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 1. **Eco-Buddy AI Chatbot**
A floating 3D-styled mascot that provides instant, AI-driven answers to eco-questions, helping users understand complex environmental issues.

### 2. **AI Plant Verification**
Users take a photo of their newly planted saplings. Our integration with Gemini AI analyzes the image to ensure it's a real plant in the ground, preventing "fake" claims and ensuring genuine impact.

### 3. **Eco-Stations (QR Integration)**
Physical or virtual checkpoints that users can "scan" (using the built-in holographic scanner) to prove they visited eco-friendly locations, earning "Eco Explorer" badges.

### 4. **Personalized Dashboard**
A central hub where users track their "EcoPoints," view their carbon footprint reduction, and manage their growing badge collection.

### 5. **Interactive Quizzes & Challenges**
Education through engagement. Users complete bite-sized lessons and real-world challenges (like "No Plastic Day") to build sustainable habits.

## 💡 The Real Problem & Our Solution

### **The Problem**
Most people feel "environmental anxiety" or find sustainability topics boring/text-heavy. There is a lack of **accountability** (knowing if someone actually did the green deed) and **incentive** (getting rewarded for good habits).

### **Our Solution: The "Play-to-Save" Model**
EcoSpark solves this by:
- **Gamification**: Turning eco-actions into a game with points, streaks, and leaderboards.
- **AI-Validation**: Using Gemini AI to provide an automated "proof of work" for planting trees.
- **Micro-Habits**: Focusing on small, daily challenges that lead to long-term behavioral change.
- **Social Competition**: Leveraging leaderboards within schools or communities to drive collective action.
